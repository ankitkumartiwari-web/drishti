About Us


Drishti is a digital platform that explores India's rich cultural and historical heritage. It provides in-depth insights into traditions, architecture, festivals, and historical events, starting with Delhi. With a seamless user experience and AI-driven chatbot integration, Drishti ensures easy access to authentic information, making history and culture more engaging and accessible.
